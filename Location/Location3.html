Nine Arch

If there’s a more beautiful scene than Sri Lanka’s Nine Arch Bridge, we’re definitely yet to witness it for ourselves. Flanked by thick jungle and tea plantations; this stone bridge stands proudly across a lush green gorge in the hill country just outside Ella. Calling it picturesque would almost be a disservice, particularly on a misty day when cloud hangs low in the valley, and the famous blue train passes slowly over the bridge, creating a moody, mystical, captivating scene.
